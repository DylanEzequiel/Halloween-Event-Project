The History of Halloween

Etymology

The word Halloween or Hallowe'en, dates back to approximately 1745. The word Hallowe'en means All Saints' Night, however there are several denominations that are given to the word Halloween, such as All Saints' Night, the Dead, Halloween or Allhalloween.

History

Gaelic and Celtic influence

Today's Halloween customs are believed to have been influenced and inherited by the popular customs and beliefs of Celtic-speaking countries, some of which have pagan roots.
